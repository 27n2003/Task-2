Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

1630 - 1680

"Freedom is a boon, which everyone has the right to receive."

Biography

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a valiant and visionary king who laid the foundation of the Maratha Empire in western India during the 17th century. Known for his exceptional military tactics, administrative skills, and deep respect for cultural values, he created a kingdom that was both prosperous and inclusive. Shivaji Maharaj was a symbol of self-rule and courage, inspiring countless generations with his leadership and indomitable spirit. His emphasis on naval power, welfare of the people, and respect for women remains unparalleled in Indian history. He was coronated in 1674 and is remembered as one of the greatest warriors and rulers in Indian history.
